Hump of a move down low, but well protected, then chill face climbing above, with a little bit of dicey gear above. Upper pitch is classic 5.4. 

## Gear
Bring the small stuff. The climbing is protectable with RPs and micro-cams, otherwise it's R. 

## Anchor
Plenty of TR opportunity here. There's a 5.8 to the left (Patty Duke), and a 5.9 to the right (Nightfall).
