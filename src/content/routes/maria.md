A classic three-pitch moderate with varied movement and a wandering first pitch.

Use the City Lights approach. Maria starts about 25' right of City Lights at the crack in a white block. The first pitch shares the Frog's Head start before traversing right on thin face climbing to a large left-facing corner. An alternate start on the Sundown corner keeps things more direct and avoids crowding on Frog's Head.

Expect a mix of face climbing, corners, and the usual Trapps route-finding decisions.
