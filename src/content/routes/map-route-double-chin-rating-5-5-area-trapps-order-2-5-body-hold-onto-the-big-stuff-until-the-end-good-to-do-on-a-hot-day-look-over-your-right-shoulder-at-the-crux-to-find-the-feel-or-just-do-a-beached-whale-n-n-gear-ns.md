Hold onto the big stuff until the end. Good to do on a hot day. Look over your right shoulder at the crux to find the feel, or just do a beached whale. 

Climb up the crack into a right facing corner up to an overhang. Step around left, and climb some beautiful rock into another broken right facing corner. Take a breath, throw some big gear in and make the pull to the bolts. 

## Gear

Single rack of cams / nuts. Bring the big blue boat anchor.
