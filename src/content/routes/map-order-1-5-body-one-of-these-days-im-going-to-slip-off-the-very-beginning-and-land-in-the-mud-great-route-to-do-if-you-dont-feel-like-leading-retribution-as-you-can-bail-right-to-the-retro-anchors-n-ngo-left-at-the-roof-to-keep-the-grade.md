One of these days I'm going to slip off the very beginning and land in the mud. Great route to do if you don't feel like leading Retribution, as you can bail right to the Retro anchors, and TR Retro, Nosedive, and No Solution. 

Climb up crack behind the tree until you get to a small overhang. Go left, then up and back right. Going straight though the overhang increases the grade bit. Climb easy stone until you get to the chains. 

If you decide to bail right to set up Retribution, stay low in the traverse, and protect your second. 

## Gear

Single rack... extra red, and green and a double length sling if you want to TR Retribution, Nosedive etc.
