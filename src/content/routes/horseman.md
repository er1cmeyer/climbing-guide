route: 'Horseman'
rating: '5.5'
area: 'Trapps'
order: 3
---
One of the standout single-pitch 5.5s at the Trapps, with surprisingly steep climbing for the grade.

Start at the crack system below the big hanging corner, between Nosedive and Laurel. Climb into the right-facing corner, clip the fixed pin at the roof, then keep moving up the enjoyable dihedral. At the tiered roof above, traverse left around the nose to a small ledge.

A direct finish through the corner is also possible and climbs a little harder, with better finger locks and strong protection.
