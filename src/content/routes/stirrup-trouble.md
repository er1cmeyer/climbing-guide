A distinctive and slightly heads-up Gunks pitch with awkward movement and sections of poor gear.

Start about 15' left of P38. Climb unprotected moves off the boulder to gain the crack, follow the horizontal left, then pull through the weakness in the small overhang. From there, weave left through small roofs before trending back up and right on the face to the top.

Descend via Radcliffe just to climbers' right, or use the Uberfall Descent.
