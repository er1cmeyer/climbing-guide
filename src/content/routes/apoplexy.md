An excellent Uberfall pitch with a little of everything: insecure face climbing, sparse-feeling gear down low, and a better-protected finish.

Start about 10' right of Horseman and head for the small loose flake. From there, either bail right to a tree or continue on the real line up the roof on the left. The crux comes on balancy moves below the good rest above the roof.

Finish by moving right to the chains.

## Anchor
Chains at the top.
