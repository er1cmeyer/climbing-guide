Climb up the sketchy but massive flake and avoid the beached whale move out right. Climb up the crack, traversing left at the small overhang to a tree. Fire up from the tree, through a ceiling and then left to bolts. 

P2 is a total easy romp, good for a beginner leader. (Straight up to bolts.) If you want to be fast, just thread the rings at the top and let your belayer lower you to the ledge. 

TR Ape call off the bottom anchor, by dropping a directional on climbers left.
