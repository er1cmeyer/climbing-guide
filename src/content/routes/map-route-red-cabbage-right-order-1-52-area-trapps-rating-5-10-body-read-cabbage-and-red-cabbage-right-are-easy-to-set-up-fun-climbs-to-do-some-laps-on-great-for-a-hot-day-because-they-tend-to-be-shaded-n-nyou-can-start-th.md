Read Cabbage and Red Cabbage Right are easy to set up, fun climbs to do some laps on. Great for a hot day because they tend to be shaded. 

You can start this climb in two ways:

1. Go up the Red Cabbage crack and then break right at the overhang OR
2. Start from the extreme right side of the block (uphill) side of the block (slightly harder)

Either way, climb the typical north facing gunks wall style climbing to the middle of the block. (Overhanging, jagged and kinda juggy)

## Anchor
Gear anchor utilizing the massive boulder on the north east corner of the Gerdie block. Purple and green cams.
