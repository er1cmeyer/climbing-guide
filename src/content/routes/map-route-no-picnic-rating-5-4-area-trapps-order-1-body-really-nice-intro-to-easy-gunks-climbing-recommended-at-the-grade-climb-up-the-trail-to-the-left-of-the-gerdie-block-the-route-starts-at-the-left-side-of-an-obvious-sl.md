Really nice intro to easy Gunks climbing. Recommended at the grade. Climb up the trail to the left of the Gerdie block. The route starts at the left side of an obvious slab, underneath a left facing corner. Beware a loose block at the top of the corner. No real danger here, but it can be unsettling for a beginner climber.
