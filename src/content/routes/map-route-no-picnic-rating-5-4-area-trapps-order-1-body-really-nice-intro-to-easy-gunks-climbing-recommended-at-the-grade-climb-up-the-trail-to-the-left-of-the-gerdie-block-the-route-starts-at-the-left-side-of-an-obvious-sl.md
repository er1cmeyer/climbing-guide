Really easy intro to Gunks climbing. Beware a loose block at the top of the corner. No real danger here, but it can be unsettling for a beginner climber. 

## The Route

Begin at the left side of a slab up the hill from the Gerdie Block. Climb the slab into an open corner, then move right underneath an overhang. Pull through a small overhang, and then move left. Climb the path of least resistance to the chains. 

## Anchor

Bolts, TR the stuff to the right if you want some decent 5.8 pulling. Make sure to put in a directional.
