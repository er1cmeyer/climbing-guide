Really nice intro to easy Gunks climbing. Beware a loose block at the top of the corner. No real danger here, but it can be unsettling for a beginner climber.

Pull through a small overhang, and then move up and left to the chains. 

At the little overhang, you can drop a nut right in the lip if you want to be fancy.
