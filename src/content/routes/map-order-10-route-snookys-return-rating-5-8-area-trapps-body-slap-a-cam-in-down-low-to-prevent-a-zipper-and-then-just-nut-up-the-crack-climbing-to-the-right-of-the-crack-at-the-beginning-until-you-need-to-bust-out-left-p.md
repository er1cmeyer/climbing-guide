Slap a cam in down low to prevent a zipper, and then just nut up the crack, climbing to the right of the crack at the beginning, until you need to bust out left... Pin at the upper crux. 

## Anchor 
Bolts. Scurry right if you want to TR Friends and Lovers. Directional uses a red 1 and pin on a double length sling. 

## Gear 
Nuts, single rack, no boat anchor... Reserve the red #1 for the directional on Friends and Lovers if thats what you want to do.
