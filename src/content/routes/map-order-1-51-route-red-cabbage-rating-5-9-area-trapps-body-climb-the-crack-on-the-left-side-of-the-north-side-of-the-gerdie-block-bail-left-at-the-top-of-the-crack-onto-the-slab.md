Climb the crack on the left side of the north side of the Gerdie block. bail left at the top of the crack onto the slab. Use the backside of the block on top as a TR anchor, and you've got Red Cabbage Right set up. 

Use the rap slings to get down, or walk off the side. 

\## Gear

Double up on the finger sized cams, purple and green needed for the TR anchor, along with a quad length sling.
