A steep Uberfall classic that follows a left-facing corner into a right-facing corner, capped by a finger crack.

Climb the initial dihedral to the small roof on the left, clip the fixed pin, and work up the short hand crack. The crux is the bulging finger crack above. The locks are good, and the position gets better as you go.

Traverse left to the anchor and rappel with a single rope.

## Anchor
Bolt anchor at the top.
