Easy to set up on TR now that there are rap rings at the top of the Gerdie block. Not really recommended as a lead, kinda reachy, slabby. 

Climb up the Gerdie trending left to a tiny seam to the left of the piton. Step right, and fire up through imaginary footholds to better climbing above.
